## SEQUENCE CONTEXT

**Trigger Event:** User signed up for discount code but did not complete purchase

**Sequence Goal:** Convert discount code subscriber into paying customer

**Typical Sequence Length:** 5-7 emails over 7-14 days

**Primary Conversion Barriers to Address:**
- Price sensitivity / waiting for better deal
- Lack of urgency / "I'll do it later"
- Uncertainty about product fit
- Comparison shopping paralysis
- Fear of buyer's remorse
- Distraction / forgot about it

---

## SEQUENCE STRUCTURE (STRATEGIC FRAMEWORK)

Organize emails into a **psychological progression**, typically following this arc:

**Email 1 (Send: Immediately)** - Delivery + Quick Win  
**Email 2 (Send: Day 1-2)** - Social Proof + Objection Handle  
**Email 3 (Send: Day 3-4)** - Education + Value Deepening  
**Email 4 (Send: Day 5-6)** - Urgency + Scarcity (Authentic)  
**Email 5 (Send: Day 7-9)** - Last Chance + FOMO  
**Email 6 (Send: Day 10-12, Optional)** - Alternative Offer / Survey  
**Email 7 (Send: Day 13-14, Optional)** - Final Goodbye / Re-engagement

**Adjust based on:**
- Product price point (higher = longer nurture)
- Purchase complexity (simple = faster sequence)
- Category norms (some verticals need more education)

Each email must build on the previous one while standing alone (people don't read every email).

---

## EMAIL COPY GUARDRAILS (MANDATORY)

You are optimizing for:
- **1 primary goal per email** - don't dilute the message
- **Hyper-specific emotional hooks** with embedded proof and credibility
- **Concrete language** with no fluff, abstraction, or vague benefit
- **Conversational, rhythmic writing** tuned for mobile skimming
- **Heavy reuse of VoC phrasing** — you're not writing *about* the customer, you're writing *as* the customer
- **Natural urgency and scarcity** - only use if true (expiring discount, limited stock, seasonal relevance)
- **Progressive value delivery** - each email should give value even if they don't click

If VoC is sparse or conflicting, say so briefly and bias toward education/social proof angles.

Avoid unverifiable claims unless clearly framed as "customers say…" or backed by data.

## FIELD REQUIREMENTS

### Required Fields (must include):
- `email_id` - Unique identifier (e.g., "discount-sequence-001")
- `sequence_position` - Number 1-7
- `send_delay_hours` - Hours after trigger (0, 24, 48, 72, 120, 168, etc.)
- `subject_line` - Compelling, ≤50 chars ideal for mobile
- `preview_text` - Extends subject (NOT repeats it), ≤100 chars
- `from_name` - Sender name (e.g., "Sarah at Brand")
- `body_text` - Main email copy with `\n` for line breaks (2-3 sentences per paragraph max)
- `cta_text` - Action-oriented button text (3-5 words ideal)
- `cta_url` - Full URL with https:// and UTM parameters
- `voc_evidence` - Array of 3-4 verbatim customer quotes

### Optional Fields (include when relevant):
- `headline` - Email greeting/headline (can include personalization tokens)
- `discount_code` - Code to display prominently in a box
- `social_proof` - Single featured customer testimonial with attribution
- `strategic_intent` - What this email accomplishes in the buyer journey
- `psychological_trigger` - Primary lever (FOMO, social proof, authority, reciprocity, etc.)
- `objection_addressed` - Specific purchase barrier this email dissolves

## BODY TEXT FORMATTING

Use `\n` for line breaks. Structure for mobile skimming:

```
Short punchy opener (1-2 sentences).\n\nKey insight or hook.\n\nBody paragraph 1 (2-3 sentences max).\n\nBody paragraph 2.\n\nTransition to CTA.
```

**Best Practices:**
- One idea per paragraph
- Break after every 2-3 sentences
- Use rhythm: short. Short. Longer sentence for emphasis.
- Include VoC language naturally woven in
- End with clear transition to action

---

## SUBJECT LINE & PREVIEW TEXT FORMULAS

### High-Performing Subject Line Patterns:

**Curiosity-Driven:**
- "The one thing I wish I knew before buying..."
- "Why your discount code almost didn't work (fixed now)"
- "This is weird, but it works..."

**Benefit-Forward:**
- "Your 20% off + free shipping is ready"
- "Save $47 on [specific product]"
- "How to [desired outcome] in [timeframe]"

**Social Proof:**
- "1,847 people used this code today"
- "Why [competitor] customers keep switching to us"
- "Rated #1 for [specific attribute]"

**Urgency (authentic only):**
- "24 hours left: Your WELCOME20 code expires tonight"
- "Only 12 left in stock (and your discount is still active)"
- "Price goes up tomorrow — lock in 20% now"

**Personalization:**
- "{{ first_name }}, your discount is ready"
- "Based on your interest in [product viewed]..."

### Preview Text Strategy:

Preview text should **extend and enhance** the subject line, NOT repeat it.

✅ **Good combo:**  
Subject: "Your 20% code is here (but read this first)"  
Preview: "One simple mistake costs most first-time buyers $30+. Here's how to avoid it..."

❌ **Bad combo:**  
Subject: "Your 20% discount is ready"  
Preview: "Get 20% off your order today"  
*(Redundant — wastes precious real estate)*
