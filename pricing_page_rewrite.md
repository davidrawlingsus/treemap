# MapTheGap.ai — Pricing Page Rewrite

## Design Notes

**Problem with the current page:** It sells features (treemaps, CSV upload, media library). The emails sell transformation (your reviews became your best ads overnight). Anyone landing on this page from the email sequence hits a wall of SaaS jargon that sounds nothing like the voice that brought them here.

**The fix:** Every tier should answer "what does this replace?" not "what does this include?" The page should feel like Email 5 (cost-of-inaction) leading into Email 6 (last chance). Features still exist — but they're secondary to the value frame.

## FAQ SECTION (below pricing)

**"Can I really replace my copywriter with this?"**

Not entirely — and we wouldn't want you to. MapTheGap doesn't replace human creativity. It replaces the *research* and *first draft* that takes your copywriter weeks. Instead of guessing what your customers want to hear, they start with production-ready copy built from real customer language. Your copywriter goes from researcher to editor. That's a better job.

**"How is this different from ChatGPT writing my ads?"**

ChatGPT guesses. It hallucinates benefits. It writes "premium quality" and "fast shipping" because it doesn't know what your customers actually say. MapTheGap reads every review on your Trustpilot page, extracts the themes, pain points, and emotional language, then writes ads using proven viral content frameworks and direct response copywriting principles. The difference is the input — real customer words vs. a generic prompt.

**"What if I don't have many Trustpilot reviews?"**

You can upload data from any source — customer surveys, NPS responses, support conversations, social media comments. The engine works on any customer verbatim. That said, even 50 reviews contain patterns most brands have never seen. You don't need thousands. You need the right analysis.

**"Why £950/month for Pro?"**

Because the alternative is £5,500-12,000/month in agency and freelancer fees for someone to do the same job slower, with less data, and based on assumptions instead of actual customer language. We're not priced against other software tools. We're priced against the team of people you no longer need to hire.

**"Can I try it before I commit?"**

Basic gives you 5 AI generations so you can see the quality of the output before upgrading. And if you came here from one of our emails, you've already seen it — those personalised ads and email sequences were built by the same engine.

- Price points (£250 / £950 / Custom)
- Core feature set — just reframed
