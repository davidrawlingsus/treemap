FAQ

"Can I really replace my copywriter with this?"

Not entirely - and we wouldn't want you to. MapTheGap doesn't replace human creativity. It replaces the research and first draft that takes your copywriter weeks. Instead of guessing what your customers want to hear, they start with production-ready copy built from real customer language. Your copywriter goes from researcher to editor. That's a better job.

"How is this different from ChatGPT writing my ads?"

ChatGPT guesses. It hallucinates benefits. It writes "premium quality" and "fast shipping" because it doesn't know what your customers actually say. MapTheGap reads your reviews, open text survey responses, NPS verbatims, and other customer feedback, extracts the themes, pain points, and emotional language, then writes ads using proven viral content frameworks and direct response copywriting principles. The difference is the input - real customer words vs. a generic prompt.

"What if I don't have much review or survey data?"

You can upload data from any source - Trustpilot, open text surveys, NPS verbatims, support conversations, social media comments. The engine works on any customer verbatim. That said, even 50 responses contain patterns most brands have never seen. You don't need thousands. You need the right analysis.

"Why £950/month for Pro?"

Because the alternative is £5,500–12,000/month in agency and freelancer fees for someone to do the same job slower, with less data, and based on assumptions instead of actual customer language. We're not priced against other software tools. We're priced against the team of people you no longer need to hire.
